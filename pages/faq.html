Frequently Asked Questions Dry Fruits & Honey in Pakistan

Have questions about buying dry fruits and pure honey online in Pakistan? Here are clear answers to the most common concerns related to quality delivery storage and ordering.

Are your dry fruits fresh?
Yes all our dry fruits are sourced from trusted suppliers and packed fresh. We avoid long storage to maintain taste and quality.
Is your honey pure and natural?
We provide natural honey without added sugar or artificial ingredients. Each batch is selected for quality and consistency.
How do you ensure product quality?
We follow a strict process of sourcing quality checking and secure packaging to ensure every product meets our standards.
Do you deliver all over Pakistan?
Yes we offer nationwide delivery across Pakistan.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery time depends on your location but usually takes a few working days.
Do you offer Cash on Delivery (COD)?
Yes Cash on Delivery is available in most areas of Pakistan.
How should I store dry fruits?
Store dry fruits in airtight containers in a cool dry place. For longer storage refrigeration is recommended.
How should honey be stored?
Keep honey in a sealed container at room temperature and avoid refrigeration.
Can I return my order?
Returns are accepted only if the product is damaged or incorrect. You must report the issue within 24 48 hours.
Can I order gift bundles?
Yes we offer seasonal and premium gift bundles suitable for different occasions.
Why are your prices different from local market?
Our pricing reflects product quality freshness and sourcing standards. We avoid low quality bulk stock.
Do you offer discounts or seasonal deals?
Yes we regularly introduce seasonal offers and special bundles.
